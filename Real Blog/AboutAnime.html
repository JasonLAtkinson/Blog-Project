About Anime

Hi, my name is,Jason Atkinson and I am an Anime Nerd Here are my top three Anime. I find myself in some way with each character.
Bleach
Posted on 10/06/2006

Bleach follows the adventures of a teenager named Kurosaki Ichigo, who inherits his parents' destiny after he obtains the powers of a Shinigami a death personification similar to the Grim Reaper—from a young woman who is also, a Shinigami named Rukia Kuchiki.

Hero Academia
Posted on 05/04/2016

My Hero follows the tale of Izuku Midoriya. a boy born without any powers, Academies across the globe train their students to learn to fight crime with their power also know as quirks. Midoriya dreams of being able to become a super hero too, but gets bullied for his unrealistic dreams. One day, his fate changes in an unexpected meeting with the #1 Hero All Might.

Katekyo Hitman Reborn! AKA Reborn!
Posted on 10/10/200
Katekyō Hitman Reborn!, It follows Tsunayoshi Sawada, a young boy who discovers that he is next in line to become boss of the Vongola family, a powerful Mafia organization. The Vongolas' most powerful hitman, a gun-toting infant named Reborn, is sent to teach Tsuna how to be a boss.
